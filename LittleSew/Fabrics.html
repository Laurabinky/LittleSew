Sort of fabrics

There are 12 different types of fabrics we will talk about. These fabrics are:

  • Cotton
  • Denim
  • Linen
  • Lace
  • Leather
  • Satin
  • Crepe
  • Chiffon
  • Silk
  • Synthetics
  • Wool
  • Velvet

When you scroll down this page you'll find an explanation of the fabrics and wherefor you can use them for.

Cotton

Cotton is a light and soft natural fabric. The fluff comes from the seeds of the cotton plant. The process is called ginning. Then the cotton fibers is spun into cloth. From there it can either be woven or knit.

Cotton is used mostly for pieces of clothing that should be comfortable. Cotton is known for its comfortableness and durability. However cotton is not really a nature friendly fabric to make. It uses a lot of chemicals that are not good for the environment.

What do you use the fabric for

Cotton is overall used for all types of clothing pieces. From jackets and coats to undergarments and everything in between.

Denim

Denim is a strong woven cotton, it is used a lot for clothing. Denim comes in a lot of colors but the most common one is the color indigo where a part of the denim is colored. The wrap thread is dyed while the weft thread is left white.

What do you use the fabric for

Denim is mostly used for the next pieces of clothing:

  • Jeans, overalls and shorts
  • Skirts and dresses
  • Hats and jackets
  • Shoes and boots
  • Shirts
  • Bags

Linen

Linen is one of the oldest fabrics known to man. It is made from natural fibers. The strong and weightless fabric comes form the flax plant. This is stronger than cotton.

Linen is a cool, absorbent, smooth and durable fabric. It does wrinkle quickly so needs regular ironing.

What do you use the fabric for

Linen is mostly used for:

  • Suits
  • Trousers
  • Dresses
  • Jackets
  • Blouses

Lace

Lace is made from looped, knitted or twisted fabric. It is an elegant and very delicate fabric. Originally it was made from silk and linen but now it is made from wool, cotton thread or synthetic fibers. Lace has 2 layers the ground layer and the pattern. Lace is considered a luxury fabric as it takes time to make them.

What do you use the fabric for

Lace is mostly used for:

  • Wedding gowns
  • Veils
  • Nightgowns
  • lingerie

Leather

Leather is an unique kind of fabric as it is made from the skin of animals. Depending on the kind of animal the is used it will need different ways of treatment. Leather is known for being wrinkle resistant, durable and stylish.

What do you use the fabric for

Leather is mostly used for:

  • Shoes
  • Jackets
  • Gloves
  • Bags
  • Accessories

Satin

Satin, unlike most other fabrics, is not made from a fiber. It is made when every strand is well knitted. Satin is one of the three major textile weaves. This fabric is shiny, slippery and elegant on one side and matt on the other. It is also lightweight.

What do you use the fabric for

Silk is used for:

  • Wedding gowns
  • Corsets
  • Lingerie
  • Blouses
  • Skirts
  • Coats and outerwear
  • Interlining

Crepe

Crepe is a lightweight plain woven fabric. It has a rough bumpy surface that doesn't wrinkle. Most of the time it is made from cotton, silk, synthetic or woolen fibers. It is often called after its used fiber.

What do you use the fabric for

Crepe is used for:

  • Suits
  • Blouses
  • Pants
  • Shirts
  • Skirts
  • Scarves

Chiffon

Chiffon is a fabric that is usually made from silk, polyester, nylon or rayon. It is a sheer, lightweight woven fabric that is made from twisted yarn. That gives it the slightly rough feel. It can easily be dyed.

What do you use the fabric for

Chiffon is mostly used for:

  • Scarves
  • Blouses
  • Dresses
  • Prom dresses and wedding gowns

Silk

Silk comes from the silkworm's cocoon, it is known as worlds most luxury natural fabric. It is also the strongest natural fabric but it is very delicate in handling and cleaning. Like lace the process of making silk is quite expensive due to it taking a lot of time to make. The two most popular types of silk are kashmir and shantung.

What do you use the fabric for

Silk is mostly used for:

  • Wedding and evening gowns
  • Shirts
  • Skirts
  • Suits
  • Lingerie
  • Ties and scarves

Synthetics

Synthetics do not cover one kind of fabric like all the others. Synthetics are: nylon, polyester and spandex. They don't shrink during the wash like delicate fabrics and are also usually resistant to stains.

Nylon

Nylon is a synthetic fiber fully made from polymers. The fabric is known for its flexibility, strength and resilience. It is long lasting and handles wear and tear.

Spandex

Spandex is made from polyurethane. Spandex is known for its elasticity, lightweight and strength, after being mixed with several fiber types.

Polyester

Polyester is created from petrochemicals and is made by men. It is a strong, durable, wrinkle and strain resistant fabric, it is not breathable and doesn't absorb liquids well. It is designed to keep moisture away from the body.

What do you use the fabric for

Nylon is mostly used for:

  • Outerwear

Spandex is mostly used for:

  • Jeans
  • Swimwear
  • Sportswear
  • Dresses
  • Stockings

Polyester is mostly used for:

  • T-shirts
  • Trousers
  • Skirts
  • Sportswear

Wool

Wool can either be knitted or woven. It is often said to be itchy and hairy. It keeps the body warm, is durable and long-lasting. It is wrinkle free and resistant to wear and tear. It can be expensive and is harder in washing.

What do you use the fabric for

Wool is mostly used for:

  • Socks
  • Sweaters
  • Gloves

Velvet

Velvet is a soft and luxurious kind of fabric.It is associated with royalty a lot due to the complex production process. The heavy fabric has a smooth finish on one side. The quality of the fabric is determined by the density of the pile tuft and the way it is anchored in the main fabric. Velvet can be made from: cotton, linen, silk, polyester and nylon. Making it a versatile fabric.

What do you use the fabric for

Velvet is mostly used for:

  • Blouses
  • Skirts
  • Coats
  • Evening wear
