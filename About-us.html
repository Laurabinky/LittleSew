About us

We are LittleSew, but how did we come to be where we are now?

I've had the sewing 'met de paplepel ingegoten'. Even before I was born my mother has been sewing. When I was 8 I did my first real project, a little bag it was. Since that moment I have been watching whenever my mother did something hoping that one day I could become even half as good as she is.

Since 2019 I've really picked up on sewing again. Since then I made 4 things:

  • 2 dresses
  • A Cardigan
  • A skirt

What I've been working on is a pair of shorts. For these projects I drew my own patterns. That however is a subject apart.

With every project I learned something new and are able to improve the next project.
